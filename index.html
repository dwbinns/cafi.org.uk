iCafe

iCafé is for international students at Anglia Ruskin University. Come and join the ‘iCafé family’ where you’ll meet new friends and enjoy: cultural nights, games and international food. Organised by Friends International and ARU Chaplaincy.

ARU term time, Mondays 6-8pm, Cos 125, ARU.

Compass cafe

Compass Cafe is run by students from the Cambridge University Christian Union. It provides an opportunity to make friends from all over the world, practise speaking English, have fun and eat a lot of biscuits! There is also an optional chance to find out about the Christian Faith.

Cambridge University term time,
Fridays 5-7pm, Livingstones cafe,
St Andrew's St. CB2 3AR
